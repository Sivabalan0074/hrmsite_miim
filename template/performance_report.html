Questions & Answers
What is start date?
Start of assignment of the associate.
What is completion date?
Completion of assignment of the associate.
What are time sheet hours?
Hours logged by associate in online time-sheet. In case of onsite assignment, these will be timesheet hours submitted to the customer.
What are available hours?
Available Hours = No. of work days × 9 Hrs
What is Assigned work hours?
Assigned work hours are estimated hours for assigned work. This should come from the project plan or should be entered by PM.

* In case the person is onsite, working as per customer instructions then Assigned Hours = Time-sheet Hours
What is "Tools responsible for"?
These are no. of tools associate was responsible for — or in other terms no. of tools for which he/she owns the design and completed from start to finish.
It should be categorised in terms of Complex, Medium or Easy.
What is "Tools worked on"?
These are no. of tools associate owned or also tools he supported the team.
It should be categorised in terms of Complex, Medium or Easy.
What should be comments from leader?
This is feedback by the leader to associate e.g. regarding efficiency, quality, challenges, improvements required, expectations etc.
What should be comments from associate?
This is associate's experience working on the project, lessons learnt, achievements, expectations etc.
Also associate can write what he did in the project, e.g. responsibility, work scope etc.
